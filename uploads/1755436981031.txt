Back & Biceps Blast Program
---------------------------

Warm-Up:
- 5-7 minutes light cardio (rowing machine or jogging)
- Dynamic stretches for shoulders and lats

Main Workout:
1. Pull-Ups (or Assisted Pull-Ups) – 4 sets x 8-12 reps
   Rest: 90 sec
   Tip: Use full range of motion, squeeze lats at the top.

2. Barbell Bent-Over Rows – 4 sets x 8-10 reps
   Rest: 2 min
   Tip: Keep your back straight, avoid jerking the bar.

3. One-Arm Dumbbell Row – 3 sets x 10-12 reps each side
   Rest: 60 sec
   Tip: Pull towards your waist, not your chest.

4. Lat Pulldown (or Band Pulldown) – 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   Rest: 75 sec
   Tip: Control both the pull and the release.

5. Barbell Biceps Curl – 4 sets x 8-10 reps
   Rest: 90 sec
   Tip: Don’t swing, keep elbows tight to your sides.

6. Dumbbell Hammer Curls – 3 sets x 12 reps
   Rest: 60 sec
   Tip: Focus on the squeeze at the top.

7. Face Pulls (Cables or Bands) – 3 sets x 15 reps
   Rest: 60 sec
   Tip: Great for rear delts and posture, pull towards your forehead.

Cool Down:
- Stretch lats, traps, and biceps
- Hold each stretch 20-30 seconds

Suggestions:
- Perform this workout 2x per week with at least 48 hours rest between sessions.
- Progressive overload is key: increase weight gradually while maintaining good form.
- Pair with a protein-rich diet to optimize muscle recovery.
