Leg Day Power Program
---------------------

Warm-Up:
- 5 minutes cycling or treadmill walk
- Dynamic stretches (leg swings, hip openers, bodyweight squats)

Main Workout:
1. Barbell Back Squat – 5 sets x 6-8 reps
   Rest: 2-3 min
   Tip: Keep chest up, core braced, and squat to parallel or below.

2. Romanian Deadlift – 4 sets x 8-10 reps
   Rest: 2 min
   Tip: Focus on hamstring stretch, hinge at the hips, avoid rounding the back.

3. Walking Lunges (with Dumbbells) – 3 sets x 12 steps per leg
   Rest: 90 sec
   Tip: Step forward and push through your front heel for glute activation.

4. Leg Press – 4 sets x 10-12 reps
   Rest: 90 sec
   Tip: Don’t lock knees at the top, control the negative phase.

5. Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets x 10-12 reps per leg
   Rest: 75 sec
   Tip: Elevate rear foot on a bench, keep torso upright.

6. Lying Leg Curls (or Band Hamstring Curls) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps
   Rest: 60 sec
   Tip: Squeeze hamstrings at the top.

7. Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15-20 reps
   Rest: 60 sec
   Tip: Full range of motion, pause at the top for contraction.

Cool Down:
- Static stretches for quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves
- Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds

Suggestions:
- Do this workout 1-2x per week, allowing at least 3 days recovery between sessions.
- Focus on form before increasing weight.
- Add progressive overload by increasing weight or reps gradually.
