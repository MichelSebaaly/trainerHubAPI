UPPER BODY STRENGTH & HYPERTROPHY PROGRAM

Warm-Up:
- 5 min light cardio (rowing, cycling, or treadmill)
- Dynamic stretches (arm circles, band pull-aparts)

1. Bench Press
   - 4 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

2. Pull-Ups (weighted if possible)
   - 4 sets x 6-8 reps
   - Rest: 120 sec

3. Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell)
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

4. Barbell Row
   - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

5. Dumbbell Lateral Raises
   - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

6. Incline Dumbbell Press
   - 3 sets x 10-12 reps
   - Rest: 90 sec

7. Bicep Barbell Curls
   - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

8. Tricep Rope Pushdowns
   - 3 sets x 12-15 reps
   - Rest: 60 sec

Cool Down:
- Stretch chest, shoulders, and arms (hold 20–30 sec each)
- Foam roll upper back

Tips:
- Increase weight gradually while maintaining good form.
- If short on time, superset opposing muscle groups (e.g., bench press with barbell rows).
- Perform 2–3 times per week, resting at least 48h between sessions.
