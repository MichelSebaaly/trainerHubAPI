Duration: 4 Weeks — 4 days/week (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)
Goal: Build strength and muscle mass
Rest Between Sets: 60–90 seconds

Workout A (Upper Body Push)

Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 6 reps

Overhead Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 8 reps

Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 10 reps

Triceps Dips (weighted if possible) – 3 sets x 10-12 reps

Workout B (Lower Body Strength)

Barbell Back Squat – 4 sets x 6 reps

Romanian Deadlift – 3 sets x 8 reps

Walking Lunges – 3 sets x 12 reps/leg

Calf Raises – 3 sets x 15 reps

Workout C (Upper Body Pull)

Pull-Ups – 4 sets x max reps

Barbell Bent-over Row – 3 sets x 8 reps

Dumbbell Bicep Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps

Face Pulls – 3 sets x 15 reps

Workout D (Full Body Circuit)
3 Rounds of:

Kettlebell Swings – 15 reps

Push-Ups – 20 reps

Goblet Squats – 15 reps

Plank – 60 seconds
