Duration: 6 Weeks — 3 days/week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)
Goal: Lose fat, improve general fitness
Rest Between Sets: 45–60 seconds

Day 1: Full Body Burn

Bodyweight Squats – 3 sets x 15 reps

Knee Push-Ups – 3 sets x 12 reps

Glute Bridges – 3 sets x 20 reps

Mountain Climbers – 3 sets x 30 seconds

Day 2: Cardio & Core

Brisk Walk or Jog – 20 minutes

Plank – 3 sets x 30 seconds

Bicycle Crunch – 3 sets x 20 reps

Russian Twists – 3 sets x 20 reps

Day 3: Full Body + Mobility

Dumbbell Deadlifts – 3 sets x 12 reps

Wall Sits – 3 sets x 30 seconds

Seated Shoulder Press (light dumbbells) – 3 sets x 12 reps

Child’s Pose & Cat-Cow Stretch – 5 minutes
